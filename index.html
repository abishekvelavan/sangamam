Sangamam

An Echoing Symphony

An Embodiment of 17 Clubs

Sangamam is the unity of arts. The Team Sangamam consists of 17 clubs to explore the extra-curricular activities of students and to enrich their skills. During this Covid-19 pandemic situation, KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology students are conducting online webinars and various sorts of events for school and college students. E-certificates are provided for both the internal and external participants as a token of active participation.

10000+ Participants

Learning beyond the academics also play a major role in some aspects. Everyone should explore and reveal their talents out. Sangamam creates a nice platform for the students to expose their skills and innovation in extracurricular activities. Each club in Sangamam are conducting webinar with a well talented and experienced resource person. It paves the way to feed the participants with additional knowledge and lifetime experience which was shared by the speaker. Many positive feedbacks were received for the conducted webinars.

Explore! Enrich!
